Insanely delicious chocolate cake is the only way I can describe this cake made of baked beetroots and chocolate. You know that beets are very useful and fall into the category of super foods. It can be prepared in various dishes, soups, roasted salads, but in my opinion, desserts work best. It has a sweet taste, but in order to preserve its color, it is necessary to add apple cider vinegar or lemon after baking.

Some of the vitamins in beets are lost after processing but they are so many that they certainly remain enough. Beets have also helped many people reduce cholesterol and bad cholesterol levels in their system.This is because there are many soluble fibers in the root, along with betacyanin and flavonoids. Betacyanin is a compound that makes the root purple-red and also provides the root with antioxidants, which is important for our blood. This vegetable is rich in soluble fibers, which are dietary fibers that can help remove and control LDL along with flavonoids. Beets are rich in various minerals, including silica. It helps the body use calcium, which in turn gives strength to the bones. A wealth of iron is needed for all ages, especially in early childhood.
